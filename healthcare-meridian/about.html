About us

One record, three departments, no repeating your history.

Meridian Clinic was founded in 2016 to solve a simple problem: patients seeing more than one department shouldn't have to explain themselves from scratch each time.

Our approach

A shared record between departments

When a general medicine patient is referred to cardiology, the receiving doctor already has their history — no repeated tests, no lost paperwork. This single principle shapes how every department at Meridian operates.

We keep appointment slots realistic rather than overbooked, so consultations run close to their scheduled time.

What guides us

Continuity of care

Every visit, across every department, adds to one patient file.

Time respected

Appointment slots are scheduled with real buffer time, not back-to-back bookings.

Plain explanations

Diagnoses and next steps are explained in plain language before you leave the room.
